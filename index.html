Hi, I’m Rajan Bhatta, based in Kathmandu, Baneshwor. Coming from a business family, I’ve always been immersed in the world of entrepreneurship. My journey has been about blending innovation with a genuine human touch, gaining experience across multiple verticals of business in Nepal, as well as in the fields of art and content creation. Over the years, I’ve embraced diverse roles that challenged me to think creatively, lead authentically, and always keep learning. As a lifelong learner, what truly drives me is the excitement of new ideas, the power of genuine collaboration, and the endless quest for improvement—both professionally and personally. When I’m not immersed in work, you’ll find me exploring creative ventures, sharing insights on digital platforms, and connecting with people who are passionate about making a difference. I believe that every experience enriches us, and I’m always eager to discover what’s next on the horizon.
